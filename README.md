# Meta Ads Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end Power BI analytics dashboard built to analyze Meta (Facebook & Instagram) advertising campaign performance.

The dashboard helps marketing teams track campaign effectiveness, audience engagement, conversion efficiency, and budget optimization.

This project demonstrates:
- Business requirement understanding
- Data modeling (Star Schema)
- Data cleaning & transformation (Power Query)
- DAX KPI calculations
- Dashboard design
- Business insights generation

---

## Business Problem
Marketing teams need visibility into:

- Campaign reach
- Click performance
- Conversion effectiveness
- Audience behavior
- Geographic engagement
- Ad format performance
- Budget utilization

Without centralized reporting, decision-making becomes slow and inefficient.

---

## Objective
Build an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides actionable insights for digital marketing campaign optimization.

- Story
- Carousel
- Image

---

## Key Business Insights
- Strong awareness and engagement performance
- Lower purchase funnel efficiency
- Females aged 18–30 show strongest engagement
- India & US are top-performing markets
- Video ads outperform other formats
- Afternoon/evening campaigns perform best
